We introduce SeaLLMs-Audio, the multimodal (audio) extension of the SeaLLMs (Large Language Models for Southeast Asian languages) family. It is the first audio large language model (LLM) designed to support multiple Southeast Asian languages, including Indonesian (id), Thai (th), and Vietnamese (vi), alongside English (en) and Chinese (zh). Trained on a large-scale audio dataset, SeaLLMs-Audio demonstrates strong performance across various audio-related tasks, such as automatic speech recognition (ASR), speech-to-text translation (S2TT), and voice-based interactions. As a significant step toward advancing audio LLMs in Southeast Asia, we hope SeaLLMs-Audio will benefit both the research community and industry in the region. The key features of SeaLLMs-Audio include:

  • Multilingual: the model support more than 5 languages, including üáÆüá©Indonesioan, üáπüá≠Thai, üáªüá≥Vietnamese üá¨üáßEnglish, üá®üá≥Chinese.
  • Multimodal: the model support flexible input format, such as audio only, text only and audio with text.
  • Multi-task: the model support voice chat, and various audio/speech analysis tasks.

Performance: SeaBench-Audio

Due to the absence of standard audio benchmarks for evaluating audio LLMs in Southeast Asia, we have manually created a benchmark called SeaBench-Audio. It comprises nine tasks:

  • Tasks with both audio and text inputs: Audio Analysis (AA), Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR), Speech-to-Text Translation (S2TT), Speech Emotion Recognition (SER), Speech Question Answering (SQA), and Speech Summarization (SS).
  • Tasks with only audio inputs: Factuality, Math, and General.

We manually annotated 15 questions per task per language. For evaluation, qualified native speakers rated each response on a scale of 1 to 5, with 5 representing the highest quality. Please note that SeaBench-Audio is still under development, and we welcome contributions from the community.

We compare the performance of SeaLLMs-Audio with recent audio LLMs with similar sizes, including Qwen2-Audio-7B-Instruct (Qwen2-Audio), MERaLiON-AudioLLM-Whisper-SEA-LION (MERaLiON), llama3.1-typhoon2-audio-8b-instruct (typhoon2-audio), and DiVA-llama-3-v0-8b (DiVA).
